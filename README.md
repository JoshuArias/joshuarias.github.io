Privacy Policy – SATIO
Last updated: January 2026
SATIO (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is a rewards platform that allows users to earn sats by viewing sponsored content. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared when using the SATIO mobile application.
1. Information We Collect
SATIO collects only the minimum data necessary to operate the app:
Device identifier (anonymous, non-personal)
Reward activity data (number of rewards earned per day)
Ad interaction data (via Google AdMob)
SATIO does not collect:
Names
Email addresses
Phone numbers
Wallet addresses
Location data
Contacts or photos
2. How We Use Information
The collected information is used solely to:
Track reward limits and balances
Prevent abuse and fraud
Deliver sponsored content
Improve app stability and performance
3. Advertising
SATIO uses Google AdMob to display sponsored content.
Google AdMob may collect and process data in accordance with its own privacy policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy
Users may be shown ads based on contextual or device-related information.
4. Rewards and Withdrawals
SATIO provides rewards in the form of sats for viewing sponsored content.
Rewards are not guaranteed
SATIO does not provide mining services
Withdrawals are optional and subject to limits and verification
5. Data Sharing
SATIO does not sell or share personal data with third parties.
Data may be shared only:
When required by law
To prevent fraud or abuse
With service providers strictly necessary to operate the app (e.g., advertising services)
6. Data Retention
Reward and usage data is retained only as long as necessary to operate the service and comply with legal obligations.
7. Children’s Privacy
SATIO is not intended for children under the age of 13.
We do not knowingly collect data from children.
8. Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
9. Contact
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us at:
Email: support@satio.app
